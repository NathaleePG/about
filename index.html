Nathalee Guevarra

NPPG

"Nathalee is 3rd year university student studying at the University of Toronto, studying her favourite subject biology. With her growing passion for science in health, environment, ecology, evolutionary biology and general biology, it is quite easy to catch her reading articles and watching countless documentaries during her spare time. Besides her dreams as an aspiring scientist, Nathalee also loves cooking, baking, drawing, playing music and writing. Her other timeless obsessions are the colour pink, oolong tea, asian and indian food, beauty, fashion and photography.

Nathalee believes that hard work, positivity, gratitude and love are the keys to success in life and does her best to stand ever so evolving life around them. But above all, her number one favourite is her best friend and partner in crime. Teaming up with him to create this blog, not only is it a way to express her passion in beauty and lifestyle but she also believes that this could be her platform to bring her developing knowledge and growing wisdom into the world to inspire people with the relevance and purpose that science pertains to in everyday life."
